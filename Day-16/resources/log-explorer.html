The concept:

This simulator models how container standard output streams flow from host node filesystems, through Fluent Bit parsing/metadata enrichment steps, and finally to storage engines (Loki/Elasticsearch).

Next Steps:

1. In the **Ingestion Console** (left), click **"Generate Log"** on any pod. Notice the raw log entry streaming into the **Container Standard Output Terminal** (center).
2. Click on any log line in the terminal to inspect the internal **Fluent Bit processing stages** (below the terminal).

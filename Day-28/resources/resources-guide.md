## 2. Recommended Reading & Reference Manuals

* **Designing Data-Intensive Applications** (Martin Kleppmann) - Essential reading for stateful workload topology designs.
* **Site Reliability Engineering** (Google Book) - Teaches SLO/SLI setting, alert tuning, and postmortem methodologies.
* **Kubernetes Patterns** (Bilgin Ibryam & Roland Huß) - Comprehensive review of design patterns including Sidecars, Operators, and Adapters.

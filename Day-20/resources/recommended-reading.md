# 📚 Recommended Reading & Learning Resources

To supplement your learning on Day 20, explore these references and standards:

---

## 🌐 Official Documentation & Specifications

1. **The GitOps Principles (OpenGitOps):**
   * [OpenGitOps Specification](https://opengitops.org/)
   * Understand the 4 core tenets: Declarative, Versioned & Immutable, Pulled Automatically, and Continuously Reconciled.

2. **ArgoCD Core Project:**
   * [ArgoCD Official Docs](https://argo-cd.readthedocs.io/)
   * Read details on [Sync Waves and Hooks](https://argo-cd.readthedocs.io/en/stable/user-guide/sync-waves/) and [ApplicationSets](https://argo-cd.readthedocs.io/en/stable/user-guide/application-set/).

3. **Flux v2 Project:**
   * [Flux Core Documentation](https://fluxcd.io/flux/)
   * Explore the [GitOps Toolkit Components](https://fluxcd.io/flux/components/) diagram.

4. **Kustomize Engine:**
   * [Kustomize Declarative Application Management](https://kustomize.io/)
   * Understand how overlays and bases are built.
